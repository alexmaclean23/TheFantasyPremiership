ABOUT

The Fantasy Premiership is an ESPN redraft league, split into three eight-team divisions with yearly promotion and relegation. The divisions feature independent player pools for management, but are commissioned collectively and observe identical rulesets.

Follow the links below to access up-to-date league information, league bylaws, league members, and the league membership application.

SEASON

The Fantasy Premiership runs yearly seasons from August to January, drafting prior to NFL Week 01 and concluding the playoffs during NFL Weeks 16-17. The information referenced below may not be available during offseason months.

Follow the links below to access real-time matchup scoreboards, division standings, placement projections, team schedules, and all transaction activity.

HISTORY

The Fantasy Premiership aggregates data from each division yearly, resulting in robust career performance metrics for all past and present members. In tandem with snapshot views of past seasons, the league's history can be preserved in extreme detail.

Follow the links below to access season-sorted career statistics, division tables, past drafts, and a photo gallery.

SOCIAL

The Fantasy Premiership facilitates communication both within and between league divisions via a private Discord server, to which league members are individually invited. At this time, the server is not open to nonmembers under usual circumstances.

League buy-ins and pay-outs are coordinated through Venmo, with a dedicated account operated by the league commissioner. All transactions are performed by Venmo's request feature to ensure proper payment amounts and destinations.

Follow @TheFantasyPremiership on Twitter for league updates and season commentary.
